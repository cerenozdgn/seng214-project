Johnny Depp & Alice Cooper & Joe Perry

The name Hollywood Vampires is a heirloom from one of the wildest days in rock music history. This team is made up of rock stars who are regulars of bars in Los Angeles.

Alice Cooper is one of the captains of this team in the early 70s. A long list started with Keith Moon, the drummer of The Who, one of the most festive men in rock history. The team had regular guests from Jim Morrison to John Lennon. It's been a long time since those crazy days ended with losses and aging. Alice Cooper keeps distance from alcohol, Keith Moon dies. The other players of the team are either not with us or they have withdrawn into their shells..

40 years later, in 2015, Alice Cooper is bringing the name HOLLYWOOD VAMPIRES back to life by taking Aerosmith's Joe Perry and Hollywood legend JOHNNY DEPP with him. "Super Group", which entered the top 50 on the Billboard 200 list with the album they released that year, will meet with their fans all over the world throughout 2023 with their "RISE" album released in 2019.
